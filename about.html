About The New Civil War

Purpose

This app helps users discover their civic role, track their fitness, learn history, and test their knowledge — all offline and stored locally.

Privacy

All data is stored on your device only. Nothing is uploaded or shared unless you choose to manually export or post your results.

Offline Layers

The app is built in layers: identity, role assignment, tests, history, run tracking, and badges — all functioning without internet.

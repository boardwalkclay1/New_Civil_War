Glossary

Every major term used across the entire system.

Role Number

A number from 1–15 representing your core civic role. Example: 1 = Signal Runner, 14 = Care Anchor.

Role Name

The title of your assigned civic function (e.g., Navigator, Quartermaster, Mediator).

Category

The major domain your role belongs to. The app uses 12 categories:

  • Information Flow
  • Supplies & Resource Management
  • Terrain & Movement
  • Observation & Monitoring
  • Documentation & Memory
  • Maps & Spatial Intelligence
  • Movement & Guidance
  • Relationships & Tension Management
  • Training & Skill‑Building
  • Media & Evidence
  • Systems & Infrastructure
  • Health & Support
  • Connection & Networks

Subcategory

A letter from A–E that defines your specialization inside a role. Example: 1C = Field Relay.

Role ID

A combination of your Role Number and Subcategory Letter. Example: 9B or 3E.

Assessment State

The internal system that tracks your progress, test scores, page completion, flags, and attempts.

Assessment Pages (1–4)

The four pages of the Find Your Role assessment:

  • Page 1: Interests & Physical Activity
  • Page 2: Pressure, Ethics, Consistency
  • Page 3: Skills, Tools, Tech
  • Page 4: Scenarios & Group Behavior

Lie Flags

A hidden counter that increases when your answers contradict each other.

Answer‑Change Flags

A counter that increases when you change key answers during the assessment.

Attempts

The number of times you’ve submitted the assessment or taken tests.

Math Module

A set of arithmetic, algebra, and geometry tests that measure numerical reasoning.

Science Module

Tests covering states of matter, elements, and combined science reasoning.

Reading Module

A comprehension test that evaluates your ability to extract meaning from text.

Badge

A distinction earned by passing math or science tests with high scores.

Test Review Page

A page that shows your score after completing a test.

Test Results Loader

A script that loads your test history and best scores.

Run Tracker

A module that records your 1‑mile time, logs improvements, and stores your best run.

Run History Loader

A script that loads your past run times and displays your progress.

Subcategory Page

A dedicated page that explains your subcategory’s meaning, skills, impact, and training modules.

Training Modules

Recommended exercises, readings, or drills that strengthen your subcategory.

Storage Manager

The system that saves your data locally on your device.

Local‑Only Data

All data is stored on your device only. Nothing is uploaded or synced.

Service Worker

Enables offline mode and PWA installation.

PWA

Progressive Web App mode that lets you install the app on your device.

Theme

The visual style of the app. Includes default, space, and future themes.

Theme Manager

A script that switches themes and saves your preference.

Hamburger Menu

The slide‑out navigation menu used across the app.

Share Card

A generated card that displays your role, subcategory, and achievements.

Share Card Generator

The script that builds your shareable role card.

History Loader

Loads historical stories and civic parallels for your role.

Role Finder

The multi‑page assessment that determines your Role ID.

Role Finder Engine

The logic that calculates your role based on tests, answers, skills, and behavior.

Scenario Engine

The system that evaluates your responses to real‑world situations.

Profile Page

Displays your role, subcategory, test scores, run times, and badges.

Dashboard

A hidden panel showing attempts, flags, test scores, and role status.

Dashboard Icon

The glowing symbol that opens the dashboard panel.
